# VBWD Platform — Overview

VBWD is an all-in-one SaaS commerce and engagement platform that lets a business
run its entire digital storefront, content, bookings, and customer conversations
from a single system. Instead of stitching together a CMS, a shop, a booking
tool, a help desk, an email marketing suite, and a payments stack, a company runs
all of it on VBWD — with one login, one billing relationship, one support team,
and one set of customer data.

## What VBWD does

- **Commerce & subscriptions.** Sell one-off products, digital goods, tariff
  plans, add-ons, and token bundles. Built-in checkout, tax handling
  (netto/brutto, per-country VAT), multi-currency, coupons, and discounts.
- **CMS & content.** A full content management system with pages, posts, a media
  gallery, SEO controls, layouts, and reusable widgets. Marketing teams ship
  landing pages without a developer.
- **Booking.** Sell bookable resources and time slots — rooms, services,
  consultations — with availability rules and capacity.
- **Conversations & AI.** A built-in chat platform with a public website widget,
  plus AI-powered assistants (a sales consultant, a support bot, content
  generation) that know your catalog and your documentation.
- **Marketplace (planned).** Vendors publish, sell, and withdraw earnings; a
  token economy rewards activity.

## Who VBWD is for

Solo founders and small teams start on the entry plans and grow into Pro. But
VBWD's real strength shows at scale: multi-team organizations, agencies running
many client sites, and enterprises that need SSO, audit logs, an SLA, and a
named success manager. For those organizations the answer is **VBWD Enterprise**
— the plan that unlocks the entire platform with no limits and white-glove
support.

## Why companies consolidate onto VBWD

1. **Lower total cost of ownership.** Replacing five or six separate SaaS
   subscriptions with one platform typically cuts licensing spend and removes the
   integration tax of keeping disconnected tools in sync.
2. **One source of customer truth.** Orders, content, bookings, and conversations
   share the same customer records — no more reconciling exports between tools.
3. **Faster execution.** Marketing, sales, and support work in one place, so a
   campaign, a landing page, a discount, and a follow-up conversation ship in
   hours, not weeks.
4. **Extensible by design.** A plugin architecture means new capabilities (new
   payment providers, AI bots, verticals) drop in without re-platforming.

VBWD is licensed under BSL 1.1 and ships as a Community Edition (self-hosted) and
a Marketplace Edition (cloud). Enterprise customers run the cloud edition with
