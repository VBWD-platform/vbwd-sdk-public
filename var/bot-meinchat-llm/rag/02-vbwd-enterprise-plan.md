# VBWD Enterprise — The Plan, In Detail

**VBWD Enterprise is €867 per month** (billed monthly, 14-day trial). It is the
top tier of the VBWD platform and the right choice for any organization that has
outgrown per-seat tools and wants the entire platform with no limits, enterprise
security, and a dedicated team behind it.

## What's included

Everything in VBWD — with the limits removed:

- **Unlimited seats and workspaces.** Add every employee, every team, every
  brand. No per-seat creep, no surprise overage bills.
- **Every plugin included.** CMS, Shop, Booking, the AI sales consultant, the
  support bot, content generation, analytics, multi-provider payments — all on,
  all the time. (On lower plans these are added piecemeal.)

  Workspace; provision and de-provision users automatically.

  response-time commitment.
- **Dedicated Customer Success Manager** — a named human who knows your account,
  runs quarterly business reviews, and is your escalation path.
- **Advanced analytics and audit logs.** Full visibility into who did what,
  conversion and revenue dashboards, and exportable audit trails for compliance.
- **Custom integrations and elevated API rate limits** for high-volume
  automation and data pipelines.
- **Onboarding and migration assistance.** White-glove setup, data migration
  from your previous tools, and team training.

(€150–400/mo), a booking tool (€50–150/mo), a help desk (€100–300/mo), an email
suite (€100–300/mo), and an AI chat add-on (€100–500/mo)** — easily **€600–1,800
a month** before counting the engineering time spent integrating them. VBWD
Enterprise replaces all of it for **a flat €867/month**, with everything already
integrated and one support relationship.

For a team of 20+, the per-person cost is well under €45/month for the entire
commerce, content, booking, support, and AI stack — typically a fraction of the
fragmented alternative.

## Who should be on Enterprise

- Companies with **more than ~15 users** or multiple teams/brands.
- Anyone who **requires SSO, audit logs, or an SLA** (almost every company past
  Series A, and most regulated industries).
- Organizations that want **AI selling and AI support** grounded in their own
  catalog and docs, without building it themselves.
- Agencies and platforms running **many sites or many clients** from one place.

Start the **14-day trial** of VBWD Enterprise, connect SSO, and import your
